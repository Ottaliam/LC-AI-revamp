About

About LC Labs has been exploring how to use emerging technologies to expand the use of digital materials since our launch in 2016. Library collections are incredibly diverse and current AI tools do not have full capabilities to process information accurately. New AI tools are emerging rapidly, making open testing, collaboration, and shared learning essential. To ensure long-term, authoritative resources, we must develop AI-specific quality standards while fostering experimentation and cross-collaboration.

Real-life experiments and research have shown the risks and benefits of using AI in libraries, archives, and museums (LAMs). To navigate these outcomes, LC Labs is developing a planning framework to support the responsible exploration and potential adoption of AI at the Library.

At a high level, the framework includes three planning phases:

  • Understand
  • Experiment
  • Implement
